Red Apples

Red apples are delicious and come in various varieties such as Fuji, Gala, and Red Delicious.

Green Apples

Green apples are known for their crispness and tart flavor. Varieties include Granny Smith and Golden Delicious.

Yellow Apples

Yellow apples are sweet and refreshing. Popular choices include Honeycrisp and Yellow Newtown.
